# Vision — RexPet

## Problem
Pet owners want unique, artistic representations of their beloved animals but custom pet portraits are expensive (€100-500+), slow (weeks), and require finding a skilled artist.

## Solution
RexPet uses AI to transform any pet photo into a stunning artistic portrait in seconds. Museum-quality digital art at a fraction of the cost, with optional physical prints shipped EU-wide.

## Target User
- **Primary**: Pet owners aged 25-55, EU-based, digitally comfortable
- **Secondary**: Gift buyers looking for unique, personalized presents
- **Tertiary**: Social media enthusiasts wanting shareable pet content

## Value Proposition
- **Instant**: Results in under 60 seconds
- **Affordable**: Starting at €4.99 for 2 portraits
- **Multiple Styles**: Oil painting, watercolor, pop art, renaissance, and more
- **High Quality**: 2K resolution output
- **Print Ready**: Optional museum-quality canvas prints

## Revenue Model
- Credit-based: purchase packs of credits (1 credit = 1 portrait)
- Physical prints: premium margin on canvas prints (€79/print)
- No subscriptions — simple one-time purchases
